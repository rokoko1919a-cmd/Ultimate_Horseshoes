# Horseshoes

This mod based on Hyperpigeon's adds five tiers of horseshoes to the game!
They are enchantable, boost speed and jump on your mounts, and give a bit of armour.
They truly render when equipped, and can be retrieved using Shears.
Horseshoes have durability, they get damaged depending on how much your horse walk (even on its own).
They are repairable using an anvil, or the mending enchantment holding it in a player's hand.

Finally, you can use a Smithing template to upgrade your Diamond Horseshoes to Netherite!

## Mounts

Horses variants (Regular, Zombie, Skeleton), Mules and Donkeys can wear Horseshoes.

## Horseshoe Types

There are **five** types of Horseshoes: Copper, Iron, Gold, Diamond and Netherite.

![Copper, Iron, Golden, Diamond and Netherite Horseshoes](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/z4gIGraY/images/dc73a31c5248d044b696bcf4ac9fc2b936644d4b.png)

1. Copper Horseshoes: increases speed by **2 blocks per second**, jump by **0.5 block** and provides  **0.5 bar of armor**.
2. Iron Horseshoes: increases speed by **2 blocks per second**, jump by **0.5 block** and provides  **1 bar of armor**.
3. Golden Horseshoes: increases speed by **4 blocks per second**, jump by **2 block** and provides  **0.5 bar of armor**.
4. Diamond Horseshoes: increases speed by **3 blocks per second**, jump by **1 block** and provides  **1 bar of armor**.
5. Netherite Horseshoes: increases speed by **3 blocks per second**, jump by **1 block** and provides  **1.5 bar of armor**.

Their Durabilities match the vanilla Boots.

## Obtaining horseshoes

You can acquire Copper, Iron and Diamond Horseshoes by trading with Armorer villagers or by looting chests in the Overworld. Golden Horseshoes can be found in chests inside Nether fortresses and bastions. Netherite Horseshoes can be made using a Smithing Template.

## Enchanting

Players can use anvils to apply Boot enchantments to Horseshoes for greater utility. For example, you can apply the Frost Walker enchantment and enable your horse to easily cross bodies of water.
Unbreaking will make your Horseshoes last longer, and Mending will allow you to repair them using your experience if you retrieve them. This is real blacksmith work right there!
